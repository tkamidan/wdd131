Overview

Purpose

The plan is to create a website that can help people to find more information about disc golf. This information includes talking about the different discs that are available as well as some tips on how to get better at the game of disc golf. There could also be other things like news for disc golf.

Audience

My audience I believe is everyone. This is because it could be for the more hardcore disc golfers and be a great to see any disc golf news that they may want. It could be good for new players to because it could tell you which type of discs are the best for people who are just beginning.

Dynamic elements

I plan on using blog style posts for each of the news items in JavaScript, I also plan on using a form for any questions, and I also plan on using a sorting system for all the discs that are available.
